About Us

Ramakrishna Mission Vidyalaya, Coimbatore, is a major educational centre of the well-known Ramakrishna Mission. Founded by Sri T.S. Avinashilingam in 1930 with an investment of just Rs. 5.75, it has grown into a vast complex of educational service.
